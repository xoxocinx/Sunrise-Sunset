Sunrise & Sunset

I saw the sunrise and sunset, and both of them made me think of you in a way I can't really explain properly, but I'll try anyway.

The sunrise felt like you. It was soft, calm, and full of quiet hope, like the way you make my world feel better just by being part of it. The light slowly rising across the sky reminded me of how you slowly became such an important part of my life without me even realizing it. I kept wishing you were there with me in that moment, standing beside me in the silence, watching the sky wake up together. It would've been perfect if I could've shared that view with you, just us, no distractions, just peace and you next to me.

And then the sunset… it felt different, but still about you. It was warm, emotional, and a little heavy in that way where you don't want the moment to end. The colors fading into the horizon made me think of how much I care for you, and how even when the day ends, my thoughts of you don't. I wish you were there with me too, watching the sky change colors, leaning on me while everything slowly turns quiet. I would've just held you close and told you how much you mean to me without needing anything fancy.

Honestly, both moments made me realize something simple but real—you're in everything I see now. In the calm, in the light, in the endings, and in the beginnings. It's like the sky itself reminds me of you no matter what time of day it is.

And I want you to know, you're not leaving. Not in my thoughts, not in my heart. I love you so much to the point where even something as simple as a sunrise or sunset turns into a reminder of you. I just wish you could've been there with me, because everything feels more complete when it's you beside me.
